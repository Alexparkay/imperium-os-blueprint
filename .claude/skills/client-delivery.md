# Client Delivery - SOPs, AI Implementation, Operator Model

## Source Files (Read When Activated)

- `context/offers.md` - what was sold determines delivery scope
- `clients/<name>/STATUS.md` - the specific engagement's current state
- `memory/business/` - delivery playbooks and SOPs as they accumulate

## Core Thesis

The moat is NOT the AI technology (anyone can deploy a voice agent or an LLM workflow). The moat is the **operator model**: embedded execution partners who understand business strategy + sales psychology + revenue mechanics alongside AI capabilities.

## 3-Phase Delivery Framework

- **Phase 1: Audit & Strategy (Weeks 1-3)** - gather data, analyse, recommend
- **Phase 2: Build & Deploy (Weeks 4-8)** - build AI systems, integrations, train the client team, go live
- **Phase 3: Optimise & Scale (Weeks 9-12)** - collect data, optimise, plan the next phase

## 5-Phase Audit (Before Any AI Work)

1. Revenue & Business Model (Days 1-2)
2. Sales & Lead Generation (Days 2-4)
3. Operational Efficiency (Days 4-6)
4. Technology Stack & Data (Days 6-7)
5. Opportunity Scoring: `(ROI × 0.4) + ((10 − Complexity) × 0.3) + (Speed × 0.2) + (Readiness × 0.1)` → pick the top 3

## AI Deployment Priority Matrix

- **Tier 1 Quick Wins (Week 1-2):** phone/voice agent, email follow-up automation, proposal generator
- **Tier 2 Revenue Systems (Week 3-6):** sales coaching AI, lead scoring, content automation
- **Tier 3 Operational Transformation (Month 2-3):** customer success AI, sales enablement suite

## Positioning: Partnership vs Commodity Automation

- **Commodity shop says:** "We'll automate your emails / build you a chatbot"
- **You say:** "We're auditing your entire go-to-market, identifying where AI creates disproportionate ROI, rebuilding your processes, and helping you move 3x faster with less labour"

Price and scope follow the positioning. Sell the transformation, deliver the systems.

## Operator Model (Scaling Beyond the Founders)

- **Role:** mini-CEO embedded in the client business, owns the whole transformation
- **Compensation:** competitive base + performance bonus (set from local market rates)
- **Capacity:** 1-2 clients in active delivery, 3-5 in optimisation
- **Training arc (approx. 8 weeks):** fundamentals → AI tools → sales/GTM → delivery → client interaction → solo delivery
- **Hiring pipeline:** free content → community → paid inner circle → filter → hire
