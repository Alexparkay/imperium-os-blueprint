# People Dossiers

One file per important relationship: `<name>.md` (kebab-case). A dossier is the deep version of an entry in `memory/people.md`: who they are, full interaction history (dated, newest first), what they want, what we want from the relationship, open threads, and anything sensitive that should never leak into content or client-facing output. Promote someone here when their entry in `people.md` keeps growing; leave a pointer behind.
