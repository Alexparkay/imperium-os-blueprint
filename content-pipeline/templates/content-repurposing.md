## Long-Form Source → Multi-Platform

### Twitter/X Thread (5-8 tweets)
1. Hook tweet (contrarian take or surprising stat)
2. Context/setup
3. 3-5 key insights (one per tweet)
4. CTA tweet (subscribe, follow, link)

### LinkedIn Post (1 post)
- Opening hook (first 2 lines visible before "see more")
- 3-5 key takeaways with line breaks
- Personal reflection or opinion
- CTA

### Short-Form Clips (2-3 per video)
- 30-60 seconds each
- One strong idea per clip
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Caption overlay for silent viewing

### Blog Post Brief
- H1: SEO-optimised title
- TL;DR box (answer-first)
- 3-5 H2 sections expanding on the source's points
- Internal links to related company content
- CTA to the main offer
- Hand off using `blog-brief.md`

### Email Newsletter Excerpt
- Subject line (curiosity-driven)
- 2-3 paragraph summary
- Link to full video/article
- P.S. line with secondary CTA
