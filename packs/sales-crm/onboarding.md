# Installing the sales-crm pack: what to ask

Asked by onboarding Phase 5 (or any later session) when this pack is installed. One question at a time. Record answers in `memory/onboarding-state.md` under a `## Pack: sales-crm` heading, and route them to the files named below.

1. **"How do deals happen for you today - inbound, referrals, cold outreach, events? Rough split is fine."**
   → Context for which skill leads: outreach-heavy → cold-email-writer is the workhorse; referral/inbound-heavy → prospect-intel before calls is. Note in `context/offers.md` or `memory/business/`.

2. **"Who's in your pipeline right now? Name the live deals off the top of your head - prospect, company, and where each one stands."**
   → Seed `memory/pipeline.md` from the answer, mapping their words onto the stage vocabulary. Unknown values get `?`. This is the pack's first real win: their pipeline on one screen, today.

3. **"What does your sales motion look like after first contact - a call then a proposal? A demo? How many touches before you give up?"**
   → Shapes the Next-step conventions and the follow-up rhythm. Record as a short `## Sales motion` note at the bottom of `memory/pipeline.md`.

4. **"Cold outreach: does anything send automatically today, or does every message get your eyes first? And is there any list, tone, or claim you never want used?"**
   → Guardrails → `context/company.md`. Default posture regardless: the system DRAFTS, the owner SENDS. Banned claims/tones feed cold-email-writer's constraints.

After the answers: run `prospect-intel` on the hottest live deal from question 2 and show the one-screen brief - a dossier on a deal they care about this week beats any demo.
