# context/team/ - shared team knowledge (read-only)

Everything your colleagues share with the team lands here automatically: one markdown file per
contribution, synced from the company workspace by Imperium OS itself. Nothing to set up,
nothing to run.

Two rules:

- **Never edit or delete files here.** This directory is server-owned; the sync will re-add
  whatever is removed, and local edits are never sent anywhere. (Rule:
  `.claude/rules-import/32-org-context.md`.)
- **To add something, share it.** Use the "Share something with your team" action in the Your
  Work panel (or say "share this with the team" in chat). Your contribution appears here on
  every seat, stamped with who shared it and when.

Company-wide *truth* (what the company is, offers, people) lives separately in `context/org/`;
this directory is the day-to-day knowledge stream between seats.
