# Meridian Consulting

**One-liner:** Meridian is a 12-person ops consultancy that fixes broken back-office operations for mid-size logistics firms.

## Who we are

- **Owner / principal:** Dana Reyes (Dana)
- **Base:** Manchester, UK (Europe/London)
- **Team:** Dana (principal), 2 senior consultants, 8 analysts, 1 ops manager
- **Founded / stage:** 2019, established, growing 20% year on year

## What we do

We run 6-12 week diagnostic-and-fix engagements inside logistics companies whose order-to-cash process leaks money. We map the process, find the leaks, then either fix them ourselves or train the client's team to. Most engagements convert into a monthly retained "ops health" relationship.

## Who we sell to (ICP)

**Operations directors at £10-80M logistics and distribution firms in the UK.**

- Company size / revenue band: £10-80M revenue, 50-400 staff
- Industry: logistics, distribution, 3PL; leading wedge is refrigerated transport
- The person who buys: ops director or COO, measured on cost-per-order and SLA breaches
- Where they hang out: industry events, LinkedIn, referrals from accountants

## Positioning

- **We are:** an operations consultancy that ships fixes, not slide decks
- **We are NOT:** a software vendor, a staffing agency, or a strategy house

## Current focus

- Land 2 new diagnostic engagements per quarter
- Convert 60% of diagnostics into retained relationships
- Build the cold-chain (refrigerated) case-study library
